The Heart of Banditz: Our Cycling Team

Meet the passionate riders who make Banditz Bicycle Club more than just a store! Our cycling team is made up of dedicated, adventurous cyclists of all levels who live for the thrill of the ride. They’re not just athletes—they’re a community of friends, mentors, and teammates who inspire each other to pedal further and push limits. Join us on our next ride, and let’s create unforgettable memories together!

The Journey of Banditz Bicycle Club

Banditz Bicycle Club began with a simple mission: to share the freedom and joy of cycling with everyone. What started as a small group of friends riding through the streets of Braamfontein quickly grew into a thriving community of cycling enthusiasts. In 2021, we opened our doors in the heart of Braamfontein, offering high-quality bicycles, expert advice, and a welcoming space for cyclists of all levels. Over the years, we’ve hosted countless rides, built an incredible cycling team, and helped riders find the perfect bike to suit their needs. From late-night group rides to weekend adventures, Banditz Bicycle Club has become more than just a store—it’s a movement. We’re proud to be part of the vibrant cycling culture in South Africa, and we’re excited to continue inspiring more people to hit the road and experience the thrill of cycling.
